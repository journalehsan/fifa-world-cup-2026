Tournament overview

The {{ tournament.name }} will be the first World Cup with 48 teams in 12 groups of four, with an expanded round of 32 knockout stage. It is also the first men's World Cup co-hosted by three countries: Canada, Mexico, and the United States.

Group matches run from June 11–27, 2026, followed by a five-round knockout stage from the round of 32 to the final on July 19, 2026 at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey.

Hosts & defending champions

  • Hosts: Canada, Mexico, United States
  • Defending champions: Argentina
  • Debutants: Cape Verde, Curaçao, Jordan, Uzbekistan
